
# THE LAST LIGHT OF THE DRAGONFLIES

## The Expedition Begins
A group of young researchers set out on an expedition deep into a quiet, untouched forest.  
Their goal was simple — study rare species and understand nature a little better.  
But what they discovered was something far beyond their expectations.

---

## A Breathtaking Discovery
As they walked through towering trees and soft rays of sunlight, they stumbled upon a breathtaking sight —  
a swarm of delicate, shimmering dragonflies dancing in the air.  

These were no ordinary creatures. They were unusually vibrant, almost magical, and seemed to glow as they moved.  
Curious and amazed, the group stayed to observe them.

---

## The Sovereign King
Days passed as they studied their behavior, recorded data, and took photographs.  
Among all the dragonflies, one stood out — **the King Dragonfly**.  

It was enormous compared to the others, with wings that reflected light like glass and a soft glow radiating from its body.  
It lived near a strange, natural structure that looked almost like a home, as if the dragonflies had built a place of their own.

---

## The Forest Transformed
At night, the forest transformed.  
The King Dragonfly would illuminate the surroundings, its glowing body casting gentle light across the trees.  

The researchers sat in silence, watching in awe.  
It was peaceful, beautiful — something so rare that it felt unreal.  
They realized this species was incredibly rare, perhaps seen only once in many years.

---

## A Final Promise
Before leaving, they carefully documented everything — photos, notes, and observations.  
They promised themselves they would return soon.

---

## I. The Fading Light
Months later, they came back. But the forest felt... different.  
The air was quieter.  

When they reached the same spot, their hearts sank.  
The home of the dragonflies was destroyed.  
The glowing King Dragonfly and its swarm were nowhere to be found.

---

## Nature Changed
Instead, a new species had taken over the area.  
They resembled dragonflies, but lacked the beauty, the glow, and the life the others once had.  

The researchers stood there in silence.  
All that remained were memories... and the data they had saved.  

Nature had changed, and the light of the King Dragonfly... was gone forever.
